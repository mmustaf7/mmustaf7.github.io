DNS Records

DNS uses A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, NS, TXT records to route traffic and verify identities. Each has a specific function in resolving names and providing services.

Security Threats and Countermeasures

DNS was not designed with security in mind. Threats include DNS spoofing and cache poisoning. DNSSEC, DoH, and DoT help secure DNS queries through encryption and digital signatures.

Real-World Applications

Major providers like Google, Cloudflare, and Cisco offer DNS services with speed, privacy, and filtering. Organizations rely on internal DNS to manage access and performance.

Future Directions

DNS is evolving with encrypted protocols, blockchain-based systems like Namecoin and ENS, and AI for management. These innovations aim for more secure, decentralized DNS.
